Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about contacting and working with us.

What information should I include in my initial inquiry?

To help us respond efficiently, please include project details such as timeline, specifications, quantity requirements, and any specific challenges or goals. The more information you provide, the better we can assess your needs.

How quickly can I expect a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. Complex projects may require additional time for our team to prepare a comprehensive response.

Can I schedule a facility tour?

Yes, we welcome facility tours for potential clients and partners. Please contact us at least one week in advance to schedule a visit, as all tours require prior appointment.

Do you provide services internationally?

Yes, we work with clients worldwide. Our team has experience handling international shipping, compliance, and logistics to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of your location.

How do we begin the manufacturing process?

After initial contact, we'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project in detail. Then we'll provide a proposal outlining scope, timeline, and costs. Upon approval, we'll move into the design and engineering phase.

Are there minimum order quantities?

Minimum order quantities vary by product type and manufacturing process. We offer flexible solutions for both small and large production runs, and can discuss options based on your specific needs.
